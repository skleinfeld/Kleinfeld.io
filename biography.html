About Me

Born and raised in El Paso, TX, named after my grandfather, Saul D. Kleinfeld — a civic leader who helped shape the city’s amateur sports community.

I serve as Senior Product Manager at RazorMetrics and Vice President of the Rookie Division for the NTABL, combining product thinking with league operations and player development.

I’m an Eagle Scout with 25+ fifty-mile treks (AK, HI, NZ, CO). I love cooking, traveling, and life with my wife, Hannah, plus our two dogs and four cats.

For me, life mirrors baseball: it’s not about perfection — it’s about showing up, learning, and staying in the game.
